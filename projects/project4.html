Salon du mieux-vivre

The application of the Salon du bien-vivre is your living room in your pocket! You will find, in addition to all the practical information, conferences not to be missed and all your favorite exhibitors. Use the many features of the App when you come to the Salon du bien-vivre:

- Browse the list of exhibitors

- Do not miss a single conference with the complete program

- Easily find an exhibitor's stand thanks to the detailed drawings

- Find all the practical information of the show (timetables, prices, situation, etc.)

- Enjoy some promotions

At your fingertips and always available, the application of the Salon of the best living gathers all the useful information and necessary for a successful and fulfilling visit.
